Welcome to NITI Aayog Data Portal

At present the official data is scattered across central ministries and state government websites. This hinders the optimum utilization of available knowledge in policy formulation and informed decision making. Also, the data at government websites is often not available in ready to use format and there are few that offer visualizations to process this data into information and insights. A visual display of data makes comparisons easier and promotes a better understanding of trends. It thus becomes extremely imperative to have a web portal in the country which serves as a one stop reference point for a wide segment of users for authentic, comprehensive and latest data on socio-economic indicators along with distinguished visualizations. In this context, NITI Aayog envisages to create a web-based platform wherein data on all the socio-economic sectors at national, state and sub-state level could be accessed. This will help policy makers, researchers and other users to analyse and bring out the insights available in the data for better understanding of socio-economic condition of the country. This will enable policy makers to formulate programmes tailor made for specific area and section of the population.
